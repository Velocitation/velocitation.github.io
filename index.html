Innovating at the intersection of Science and Data

My focus lies in developing efficient data reporting infrastructures, applying machine learning to analytical problems, and creating automated solutions that enhance accuracy and productivity.

I invite you to explore my portfolio, where you’ll find projects that demonstrate my commitment to solving complex problems at the intersection of chemistry, data science, and technology.
